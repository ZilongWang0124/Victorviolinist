About Me

\

\ I am Victor Wang ( \f1 \'cd\'f5\'d7\'d3\'c1\'fa \f0 ), a passionate violinist and teacher. My musical journey began at the age of five under the guidance of Professor Wang Guan from Shenyang Conservatory of Music. I continued my studies at the University of Huddersfield, under the mentorship of the renowned violinist and educator David Milson.\

\

\ My performances include both classic violin concertos and popular works, and I enjoy sharing my love for music with students of all ages and backgrounds. I am dedicated to combining my technique and knowledge to inspire and guide students, helping them develop a deep connection to music.\
