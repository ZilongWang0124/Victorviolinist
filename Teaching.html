Teaching

\

\ I have studied violin under numerous esteemed teachers across China, the United Kingdom, and the United States, learning from the best and absorbing the strengths of diverse pedagogical traditions. My father is a professional violin teacher, and from a young age, I often assisted him in teaching his students. This long-term exposure, both through observation and hands-on experience, has given me deep insight into effective violin instruction.\

\

\ My teaching is rooted in solid technique and a strong foundation of musical knowledge. I aim to ignite each student\'92s passion and curiosity through engaging lessons, careful guidance, and personalized feedback. I am patient and encouraging, creating a supportive environment that empowers students to grow as musicians and as individuals.\
