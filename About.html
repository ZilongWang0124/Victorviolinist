About Me

\

My name is Victor Wang ( \f1 \'cd\'f5\'d7\'d3\'c1\'fa \f0 ), and I am a professional violinist dedicated to both performance and education.

\

I began studying the violin at the age of five under the guidance of Professor Wang Guan at the Shenyang Conservatory of Music. In 2022, I furthered my musical education at the University of Huddersfield in the UK, where I studied under renowned British violinist and educator David Milson.

\

My musical journey has taken me through years of rigorous training, masterclasses, and performances. I am passionate about sharing the expressive power and refined technique of violin playing with audiences and students alike, from classical masterpieces to modern works.
